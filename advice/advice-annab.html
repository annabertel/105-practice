Hey,
You're amazing.

Here you are doing the hardest part of learning to code — showing up. OHH YEAH!

My big picture advice:
TAKE CARE OF YOURSELF LIKE YOU MEAN IT.

When we are well
(physically, emotionally, and otherwise)
shit starts falling into place.
Commit to something that makes you feel whole;
Let go of something that doesn't.
Smile at the energetic rewards for your effort!
Then, guide this new source of energy
towards learning to code
...or wherever you feel compelled to guide it...
Because you know. Listen :)

Craving a quick boost? Read on.

Your brain and body are working overtime to support your coding journey — show 'em a couple minutes of love,
right from your chair...but first get up and walk around if you haven't for a while :)

Take five deliciously deep breaths

in through your nose
out through your mouth
each breath slightly more delicious than the last

Massage the back of your neck

while taking another one to two deep breaths here
then repeat for your shoulders, and temples

Loosen up your neurological highway

a gentle side bend (and a breath or two!) to each side
a gentle twist to each side
then a gentle arch forwards and backwards

Close your eyes. Set an intention. Smile a bit.

Happy Coding!
-Anna
